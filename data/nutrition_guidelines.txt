WHO Dietary Guidelines

Calories: Adults need 1800-2500 calories daily depending on age, gender, and activity level.

Protein: Should comprise 10-15% of total calories. Adults need 0.8g per kg body weight minimum.

Carbohydrates: Should provide 45-65% of total calories. Focus on complex carbs from whole grains.

Fats: Should be 20-35% of total calories. Limit saturated fats to <10% and avoid trans fats.

Fiber: Adults need 25-35g daily from fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.

Sodium: Limit to <2300mg daily to reduce cardiovascular disease risk.

Sugar: Added sugars should be <10% of total calories, preferably <5%.

Fruits and Vegetables: Aim for 5-9 servings daily for optimal micronutrient intake.

Water: 8-10 glasses daily for proper hydration and metabolic function.

Meal Timing: Regular meals help maintain stable blood sugar and energy levels.
